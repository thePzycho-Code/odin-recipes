Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2-3 slices of your favorite cheese. I recommend Sharp Cheddar and Ghouda
  • Mayo
  • Onion powder (optional)
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • Crumbled parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place your frying pan on the stove on medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of mayo on one each of the slices of bread.*optional* place a small amount of parmasan, onion or garlic powder to the mayo.
  3. Place one slice of bread, mayo side down, in the pan. Place your cheese on the bread.
  4. Place a teaspoon of water in the pan, then cover frying pan with lid.
  5. Allow cheese to melt, this should take about a minute.
  6. Uncover, place second slice of bread, mayo side facing away from the cheese on the sandwich.
  7. Flip, there should be a nice, golden brown crust on the bread.
  8. Allow the second slice of bread to toast, about 1-2 minutes.
  9. Remove from the pan, slice, and enjoy.
