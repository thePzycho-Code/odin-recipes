Description

This is a basic hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bun
  • Cheese (Optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Season the meat
  2. Step 2: Cook on the grill, smoker or in a skillet until internal temperature is 165 degrees F
  3. Step 3: Place cheese on top, allow to melt.
  4. Step 4: Serve on a toasted bun.
  5. Step 5: Enjoy.
